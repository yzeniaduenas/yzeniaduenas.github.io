Dinner | Main Course

I've made pot pie a bunch of different ways on here, but this one might be my favorite twist yet. Instead of puff pastry or a traditional pie crust, the whole thing gets capped off with a jalapeño cheddar cornbread that bakes right on top of the filling in the same skillet. The cornbread soaks up a little bit of the filling underneath while staying golden and set on top, and the sweet-heat combo from the jalapeño and brown sugar plays so well against the herby, savory chicken filling. It takes a bit of time and effort and dishes but the final product is worth it all!
